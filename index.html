The Working Genius model

A simple way to understand why work energizes or drains people.

The Working Genius model helps teams identify which parts of work bring energy and which parts create frustration. It is especially useful because it focuses on actual work, not abstract personality traits.

Wonder

Asks big-picture questions and sees potential.

Invention

Creates original ideas and possible solutions.

Discernment

Evaluates ideas with instinct and judgment.

Galvanizing

Rallies people toward action.

Enablement

Helps and supports others in the work.

Tenacity

Pushes work through to completion.
